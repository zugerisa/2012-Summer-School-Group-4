Grading Criteria :
Technical Content 50%
Oral Presentation: 25%
Final Report: 25%

Tech Content:
a) Quality of design
	i) Simplicity / robustness
	ii) Safety
	iii) Ergonomics
	iv) Production feasibility
	v) Projected performance
	vi) Part geometry
	vii) Sketches (main parts, assembly, handmade, roughly scaled)
	viii) Bill of materials (part list)
	ix) estimate of manufacturing costs
	x) Key supplier
	xi) Maintenance / repair issues

b) Design process
	i) Mission statement
	ii) Costumer requirements
	iii) Final Prod. Des. Spec
	iv) List of competitive concepts (for each function)
	v) concept selection chart

Oral Presentation:
A) Contents
	i) Consistency (20 min + 5 min questions)
	ii) Clarity of description
	iii) Completeness of information
B) Slide layout / design
c) Time management

Final Report:
A) Contents
	i) Exec.summary (~1 page, quantitative as well as qualitative)
	ii) Description of final design
	iii) Summary of key modeling / calculations / estimation
	iv) Conclusions & recommendations
	v) Appendix: User Manual
B) Writing quality
C) Layout, grammar, figures, etc.
